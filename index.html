    ABOUT

    Crystal Network Logistics, a subsidiary of the Crystal Logistics Group, focuses in China/ ASEAN Cross-border Transportation Solutions.

    With over 23 years of experience in cross-border trucking between China, ASEAN Countries (Thailand, Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, Myanmar, Malaysia & Singapore) & Hong Kong, we have become a valuable partner to many Third Party Logistics (3PL) Service Providers and Freight Forwarders, by customising different logistics solutions based on business requirements & budget.
